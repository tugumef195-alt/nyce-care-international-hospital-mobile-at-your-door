Patients & Visitors

Information to help you prepare for your visit to Pinnacle General Hospital

Planning Your Visit

We recommend arriving 30 minutes before your scheduled appointment to complete any necessary paperwork. Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, and a list of current medications.

  • Check-in at the main reception desk
  • Free valet parking available
  • Wi-Fi access throughout the hospital
  • Interpretation services available

Patient Resources

Access your medical records, pay bills online, and find helpful health information through our patient portal.

Patient Portal Download Forms

Visitor Information

We welcome visitors during designated hours to support our patients' recovery. Please note:

  • General visiting hours: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • ICU visiting hours: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM, 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult
  • Please check with nursing staff for maternity ward policies

Amenities & Services

We offer various amenities to make your stay more comfortable:

  • Cafeteria and coffee shop
  • Gift shop with flowers and gifts
  • Chapel and meditation room
  • ATM and banking services
  • Pharmacy on-site
