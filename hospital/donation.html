Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Nyce care international is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Our Tax ID is +33-5971545. You will receive a receipt for your records.

Can I restrict my donation to a specific department?

Yes! On the donation form, you can specify where you'd like your gift to be directed. If you don't select a specific area, your donation will be used where it's needed most.

How can I get more information?

Please contact our Foundation office at +256 70069788 or foundation@nycecare.org. Our team would be happy to answer any questions you may have.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Absolutely! When you select "Monthly Gift" on the donation form, your donation will automatically recur each month. You can cancel or modify your recurring donation at any time.
